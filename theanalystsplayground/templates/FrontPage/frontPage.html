Thank you for visiting my Stock Market website. I built this website as a proof of concept and to learn how to build a website; I am a faily new developer. This website is fairly extensive and covers over 9000 individual companies and ETFs and any news reports associated with these stocks and ETFs. Additionally the website covers a little over 200 crypto tickers along with any news associated with those tickers. The website also covers overs 11000 stock market indexes and over 1200 forex pairs. There is also a News Center where you can look up news from different sources and much more. All Prices and data are current up to 15min. If you create an account the website offers a watchlist that will track any stock you add to your watchlist. Additionally, signing up for an account permits access to a portfolio tracker that tracks gains and losses for any stocks in your portfolio. You can navigate to the home page here, or you can use the menu button on the top right of the screen. I hope you like the site, you can leave comments, constructive criticism and any issues that occur with the site in the Feedback section of the site.
